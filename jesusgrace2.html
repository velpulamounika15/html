NICK VUJICIC

Vujicic was born in Melbourne, Australia, in 1982 to Dušanka and Borislav Vujičić, Serbian immigrants from Yugoslavia.[6][7] Vujicic's father is a pastor.[8] Vujicic was born with tetra-amelia syndrome, a rare disorder characterised by the absence of arms and legs.

Vujicic married Kanae Miyahara on February 12, 2012. As of 2017, Vujicic and his wife have two sons and two daughters. The family resides in Southern California.
